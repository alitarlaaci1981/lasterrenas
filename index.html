Las Terrenas Kungfu Vacation & Retreat

Book Now

Join us for a transformative 6-day Wing Chun Kung Fu vacation retreat in the stunning town of Las Terrenas, located in the beautiful Dominican Republic. Each day offers a unique and exciting activity, seamlessly blending martial arts practice with cultural and natural explorations. This exclusive trip is designed specifically for Wing Chun Kung Fu practitioners, ensuring an enriching experience tailored to our shared passion.

Daily Activities

Sunday, February 9th

Welcome Dinner at Porto by Mosquito
Start your retreat with a luxurious welcome dinner at Porto by Mosquito, a restaurant located by the sea. Enjoy spectacular views and exquisite food as you meet fellow retreat participants. Dinner at 7:00 pm

Book Now

Monday, February 10th

Whale Watching & Bacardi Island Visit
Start your week with an extraordinary adventure on Monday, February 10th, as we depart from Las Terrenas to Samaná. Our journey begins bright and early at 8:30 AM with a scenic 20-minute drive through beautiful mountains, setting the stage for the day's excitement.

Upon arriving in Samaná, we'll board a boat and embark on a magical whale-watching expedition. For approximately two hours, we'll be captivated by the majestic whales as they grace us with their presence in the open waters.

After this unforgettable encounter, we'll head to the stunning Bacardi Island. Here, we'll indulge in a delicious lunch, soak up the sun on the pristine beaches, and revel in the island's natural beauty. The day wouldn't be complete without an invigorating kung fu practice session amidst the idyllic surroundings.

Join us for a day filled with wonder, adventure, and unforgettable memories as we explore the natural wonders of Samaná and Bacardi Island!

Departure: 8:30 A.M
Return: Approximately 5:30 P.M

Book Now

Tuesday, February 11th

Horseback Riding & El Salto del Limon Waterfall Visit
Embark on an adventurous journey on Tuesday, February 11th, with a horseback riding tour to the breathtaking Limón Waterfall. Participants have the option to either ride a horse or walk through the lush forest, experiencing the natural beauty of the Dominican Republic.

We'll saddle up and begin our trek through the verdant landscape until we reach the majestic 165-foot high Limón Waterfall, one of the highest and most stunning waterfalls in the country. Upon arriving at the ranch where we leave the horses, we'll be greeted with an incredible view of the cascading fall below.

We will spend approximately three hours at this picturesque location, where we'll enjoy a delicious lunch, take in the beauty of the waterfall, and of course, engage in an invigorating kung fu practice session.

Join us for an unforgettable day of adventure, natural beauty, and cultural enrichment!

What to bring:

Swimming attire, Towel,Sunglasses,Sunscreen

Departure: 8:15 AM

Book Now

Wednesday, February 12th

Grand Opening of Las Terrenas Wing Chun Kungfu
Prepare for an inspiring and memorable as we visit Wing Chun Las Terrenas Kung Fu. This special day is dedicated to honoring grandmaster Moy Ye Hop (Julian Cordero), practicing kung fu with fellow travelers, and celebrating the grand opening of the Las Terrenas Wing Chun Kung Fu School.

Book Now

Thursday, February 13th

Los Haitises and Cano Hondo visit
Our journey begins with a scenic departure from Las Terrenas to Samaná, where we will board a boat for an exciting visit to Los Haitises National Park. The drive to Samaná will take approximately 25 minutes.

Upon arrival at Los Haitises, we will spend time exploring the stunning natural beauty and rich biodiversity of the park. Afterward, we will head to Cano Hondo, renowned for its beautiful natural pools.

We'll stay at Cano Hondo for about three hours, enjoying a delicious lunch amidst the tranquil surroundings. Following this relaxing break, we have planned an invigorating two-hour session of kung fu practice with our skilled kung fu brothers.

Join us for an unforgettable day filled with exploration, relaxation, and cultural enrichment! Departure: 8:15 A.M
What to Bring: Closed tennis shoes,Swimsuit,Towels,Sunscreen,Raincoat (in case it rains)
Return: 5:30 P.M (approximately);

Book Now

Friday, February 14th (Valentine’s Day)

Playa Bonita Lunch & Porto by Mosquito Valentine’s Dinner and Party
On Valentine's Day, we will head out to La Playa Bonita, a popular beach frequented by tourists. Our day will begin around 10 AM, where we'll practice kung fu and enjoy the beautiful beach.

After a morning of beach activities, we'll head to a beachfront restaurant for a delightful lunch with stunning ocean views

Following a fun-filled day at La Playa Bonita, we'll return for a romantic Valentine's dinner at Porto by Mosquito. This restaurant is renowned for its amazing food and will feature a special show for Valentine's Day, making it a truly memorable evening.

Join us for a day of relaxation, adventure, and romance!

How To

From Santa Domingo to Las Terrenas

Taxi from Santa Domingo Airport to Bus Stop:

Google map direction:Santa Domingo Airport to bus stop for Las Terrenas

Bus Stop Address: Parada Samana - Asotrapusa El Jumazo, Santo Domingo


The bus stop is located right in front of the water. You can take a taxi from the airport to the bus stop, which costs around 15 USD.

Bus Schedule from Santa Domingo to Las Terrenas:

  • 8:30 AM
  • 9:30 AM
  • 11:00 AM
  • 1:40 PM
  • 3:00 PM
  • 6:00 PM

The bus fee is approximately 500 pesos (around 10 USD) and the journey takes about 2 hours. When you arrive at the Las Terrenas bus stop, you will need to take a taxi to your hotel.

From Las Terrenas to Santa Domingo

Bus Stop Address: 8F32+MCC, Las Terrenas 32000, Dominican Republic

Bus Schedule from Las Terrenas to Santa Domingo:

  • 5:00 AM
  • 7:00 AM
  • 9:00 AM
  • 11:00 AM
  • 2:00 PM
  • 3:30 PM

When you get off the bus at the Parada Samana bus stop in Santa Domingo, take a taxi to the airport (15 USD).

General Information

We highly encourage all Wing Chun Kung Fu practitioners to arrive in the Dominican Republic a day before the retreat begins. The journey from Santo Domingo Airport to Las Terrenas takes about three hours, including a ride to the bus stop and a bus journey to Las Terrenas.
Arriving early gives you a wonderful opportunity to settle into your hotel room, unpack, set up internet on your phone, rent a four-wheeler (ATV), shop, and explore the beautiful surroundings. This extra day will ensure you’re well-rested and ready to fully immerse yourself in the transformative experience of our retreat! View More

There are several mobile operators in the Dominican Republic that offer prepaid SIM cards, including Claro, Orange, and Viva.(We can definitely recommend CLARO since it has a wide coverage and setup is really Quick) These SIM cards can be purchased at various locations such as airports, convenience stores, and official stores of the mobile operators. In tourist areas it is almost perfect, but in the hinterland it can slow down.
It is advisable to purchase SIM cards of local providers in official phone shops. A shop assistant will help to activate and connect the selected package. Buying a SIM card in the Dominican Republic is possible by providing a passport. View More

Only prepaid data plans are sold to tourists, there are no contract data packages.
Important: do not buy SIM cards from street sellers or in souvenir stores.
