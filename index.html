Return: Approximately 5 PM
Sail through Samana Bay and witness the majestic whales with a knowledgeable guide. Visit Bacardi Island, enjoy a typical lunch, and relax on its beautiful beach.

Start: 8 AM
Experience a thrilling horseback ride through awe-inspiring countryside landscapes to the breathtaking El Salto del Limón Waterfall. Climb a remarkable 197-foot path and dive into a refreshing natural pool surrounded by a tropical rainforest.
