Join us for a transformative 6-day Wing Chun Kungfu Family vacation retreat in the stunning town of Las Terrenas, located in the beautiful Dominican Republic. This exclusive trip is designed not only for Wing Chun Kung Fu practitioners but also warmly welcomes their families to join the adventure. Each day offers a unique and exciting activity, seamlessly blending martial arts practice with cultural and natural explorations. Your family can enjoy the beauty and culture of the area while you practice and train. This enriching experience is tailored to our shared passion for Kung Fu, ensuring a memorable and enjoyable retreat for everyone.

Monday, February 10th

Whale Watching & Bacardi Island Visit
Start your week with an extraordinary adventure on Monday, February 10th, as we, along with our families, depart from Las Terrenas to Samaná. Our journey begins bright and early at 8:30 AM with a scenic 20-minute drive through beautiful mountains, setting the stage for the day's excitement.

Upon arriving in Samaná, we'll board a boat and embark on a magical whale-watching expedition. For approximately two hours, we'll be captivated by the majestic whales as they grace us with their presence in the open waters.

After this unforgettable encounter, we'll head to the stunning Bacardi Island. Here, we'll indulge in a delicious lunch, soak up the sun on the pristine beaches, and revel in the island's natural beauty. While friends and family members enjoy the island, fellow Kung Fu practitioners will seize the opportunity to focus on their training. We will have approximately two hours dedicated to practicing Kung Fu, making the most of this serene and inspiring environment.

Join us for a day filled with wonder, adventure, and unforgettable memories as we explore the natural wonders of Samaná and Bacardi Island!

Tuesday, February 11th

Horseback Riding & El Salto del Limon Waterfall Visit

Embark on an adventurous journey on Tuesday, February 11th, with your family on a tour to the breathtaking Limón Waterfall. Participants have the option to either ride a horse or take the trail through the lush forest, experiencing the natural beauty of the Dominican Republic. Fellow Kung Fu brothers, we have the option to be picked up by the tour company or we can ride to the town of El Limón before starting our tour to Salto del Limón Waterfall.

We'll saddle up and begin our trek through the verdant landscape until we reach the majestic 165-foot high Limón Waterfall, one of the highest and most stunning waterfalls in the country. Upon arriving at the ranch where we leave the horses, we'll be greeted with an incredible view of the cascading fall below.

On the way back to our starting point, we will conclude our adventure with a delicious lunch. The tour is expected to wrap up around 2:30 - 3:00 pm, giving us flexibility based on the day's flow. Then, we'll head back to Las Terrenas where the rest of the day will be ours to enjoy. Imagine relaxing on the stunning beaches, soaking in the tropical weather with your family, and having the perfect backdrop for continuing our invigorating Kung Fu training. It's the ultimate blend of leisure and practice in paradise!

Join us for an unforgettable day of adventure, natural beauty, and cultural enrichment!

Thursday, February 13th

Los Haitises and Paraiso Caño Hondo visit

Our journey with family and friends begins with a scenic departure from Las Terrenas to Sanchez, where we will board a boat for an exciting visit to Los Haitises. The drive to Sanchez will take approximately 25 minutes.

The word: Haitises comes from Arawak which was the Taino language and means high land or land of mountains, although the set of hills or "mogotes" have heights that range between 30 and 40 meters. An important aspect is the karstic geomorphological formation that determines, among other things, a system of caves such as the caves "de La Arena", "de San Gabriel" and "del Ferrocarril" - also known as "de la Línea" or "del Templo" - that contain pictographs, bas-reliefs and petroglyphs. Afterward, we will head to Cano Hondo, renowned for its beautiful natural pools.

Paraiso Caño Hondo is located in the Los Haitises National Park, in the northeastern region of the Dominican Republic. The eco-lodge is situated in the town of Sabana de la Mar, which is approximately 5.6 miles away. The lodge is surrounded by lush greenery and is known for its natural pools and waterfalls. Visitors can enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding mountains and forests, while also taking a dip in the refreshing waters.

We'll stay at Cano Hondo for about three hours, enjoying a delicious lunch amidst the tranquil surroundings. Following this relaxing break, we have planned an invigorating two-hour session of kung fu practice with our skilled kung fu brothers.

Join us for an unforgettable day filled with exploration, relaxation, and cultural enrichment!

How To

From Santa Domingo to Las Terrenas

Taxi from Santa Domingo Airport to Bus Stop:

Google map direction:Santa Domingo Airport to bus stop for Las Terrenas

Bus Stop Address: Parada Samana - Asotrapusa El Jumazo, Santo Domingo


The bus stop is located right in front of the water. You can take a taxi from the airport to the bus stop, which costs around 15 USD.

Bus Schedule from Santa Domingo to Las Terrenas:

  • 8:30 AM
  • 9:30 AM
  • 11:00 AM
  • 1:40 PM
  • 3:00 PM
  • 6:00 PM

The bus fee is approximately 500 pesos (around 10 USD) and the journey takes about 2 hours. When you arrive at the Las Terrenas bus stop, you will need to take a taxi to your hotel.

Bus Stop Address: 8F32+MCC, Las Terrenas 32000, Dominican Republic

Bus Schedule from Las Terrenas to Santa Domingo:

  • 5:00 AM
  • 7:00 AM
  • 9:00 AM
  • 11:00 AM
  • 2:00 PM
  • 3:30 PM

When you get off the bus at the Parada Samana bus stop in Santa Domingo, take a taxi to the airport (15 USD).

We highly encourage all Wing Chun Kung Fu practitioners to arrive in the Dominican Republic a day before the retreat begins. The journey from Santo Domingo Airport to Las Terrenas takes about three hours, including a ride to the bus stop and a bus journey to Las Terrenas.
Arriving early gives you a wonderful opportunity to settle into your hotel room, unpack, set up internet on your phone, rent a four-wheeler (ATV), shop, and explore the beautiful surroundings.
